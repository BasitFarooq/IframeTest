Kids2ProSports – Education & Sports

Mission Statement:

Kids2ProSports Academic & Sports mission is to focus on educational attainment and using the power and appeal of sport to positively affect our members by supporting physical and personal education, sport, and physical activity programs.

Kids2ProSports.com is a unique web based company that emphasizes dedicated synergy of academics and sport within an international environment. We strongly believe that a combination of intellectual pursuits with the physical, notably as realized through sports in all its varied forms, continually enhances our lives at every level of our growth from childhood…to professional and beyond.

Our lives are a continuum of learning, change and adaptability accompanied by the quest to succeed. It requires communication and discipline so our kids can play and have fun, creating life long memories. As our site and business evolves with an increasing audience, we invite you to grow along with us and become a member of our community.

Thanks for your support and we look forward to hearing from you.

Alan Chokov CEO/President Kids2ProSports.com Kids2ProSports and its affiliates embrace the 'athletes first, winning second' philosophy by providing platforms for education to put this philosophy to work." The following education based multi-sport organizations are supportive of our philosophy.
